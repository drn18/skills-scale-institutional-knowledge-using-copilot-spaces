# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## UX Designer

### Role Summary
UX Designers research user needs, design intuitive interfaces, and validate designs through prototypes and user testing. They ensure products are accessible, usable, and deliver a delightful user experience.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct user research and usability testing
- Create wireframes, mockups, and interactive prototypes
- Design user flows and information architecture
- Collaborate with Product Managers on feature requirements
- Work with Developers to ensure design implementation fidelity
- Maintain design systems and component libraries
- Advocate for accessibility and inclusive design

### Goals
- Improve user satisfaction and task completion rates
- Reduce friction and confusion in user journeys
- Ensure consistent, accessible design patterns across the product
- Validate designs with real users before development

### Typical Communication
- Design reviews and critique sessions
- Handoff documentation with specs and assets
- User research findings and testing reports
- Collaboration with PM and Engineering on trade-offs

---

## Technical Writer

### Role Summary
Technical Writers create clear, accurate documentation that helps users and developers understand and use products effectively. They maintain documentation throughout the product lifecycle.

### Responsibilities
- Write user guides, API documentation, and tutorials
- Maintain release notes and changelog documentation
- Create and update internal process documentation
- Collaborate with Developers and Product Managers on content accuracy
- Ensure documentation accessibility and searchability
- Review technical content for clarity and consistency
- Manage documentation versioning and publishing workflows

### Goals
- Reduce support burden through clear, comprehensive documentation
- Improve developer experience and product adoption
- Maintain accurate, up-to-date documentation
- Ensure documentation meets accessibility standards

### Typical Communication
- Documentation review requests and feedback
- Content handoff checklists from developers
- Stakeholder reviews for major documentation updates
- Collaboration on product launches and feature releases

---

## Release Manager

### Role Summary
Release Managers coordinate and oversee the release process, ensuring all release activities are properly planned, executed, and communicated. They act as the central point of coordination for deployment activities.

### Responsibilities
- Plan and schedule release windows
- Coordinate release activities across teams
- Maintain release calendar and communicate timelines
- Ensure release readiness criteria are met
- Manage release checklists and deployment runbooks
- Coordinate go/no-go decisions with stakeholders
- Track release metrics and post-release validation
- Facilitate release retrospectives

### Goals
- Ensure smooth, predictable releases with minimal disruption
- Reduce deployment-related incidents and rollbacks
- Improve release process efficiency and automation
- Maintain clear communication with all stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Release planning meetings and go/no-go checkpoints
- Release status updates and announcements
- Coordination with Development, QA, and Operations teams
- Post-release summaries and metrics reports

---

## QA Lead

### Role Summary
QA Leads define testing strategy, coordinate quality assurance activities, and ensure products meet quality standards before release. They balance thoroughness with delivery timelines.

### Responsibilities
- Define test strategy and quality standards
- Review acceptance criteria and testability of requirements
- Coordinate manual and automated testing efforts
- Manage test environments and test data
- Track defects and quality metrics
- Collaborate with developers on test automation
- Sign off on release readiness from quality perspective
- Advocate for quality throughout the development process

### Goals
- Prevent critical defects from reaching production
- Improve test coverage and automation rates
- Reduce time spent on manual regression testing
- Enable faster, more confident releases

### Typical Communication
- Test plan reviews and QA status updates
- Defect triage meetings with developers
- Release readiness assessments
- Quality metrics reporting to stakeholders

---

## Business Analyst

### Role Summary
Business Analysts bridge the gap between business stakeholders and technical teams by analyzing requirements, documenting processes, and ensuring solutions meet business needs.

### Responsibilities
- Gather and document business requirements
- Analyze current processes and identify improvement opportunities
- Create process flows, data models, and functional specifications
- Facilitate requirements workshops with stakeholders
- Validate that solutions meet business requirements
- Support user acceptance testing (UAT)
- Document business rules and system behaviors
- Track requirements traceability

### Goals
- Ensure clear understanding of business needs across all teams
- Reduce rework from requirement misunderstandings
- Improve alignment between business goals and technical solutions
- Enable informed decision-making through analysis

### Typical Communication
- Requirements gathering sessions with stakeholders
- Business requirement documents and user stories
- Process flow diagrams and data models
- UAT planning and results reporting
